<details>
  <summary>why "isatope"?</summary>
  <p>
  Isotopes are atoms of the same element with different numbers of neutrons — same identity, different structure. Stable isotope labeling is also a real technique in mass-spec–based metabolomics, used to trace how molecules move through a system. Also, yes: <strong>isa</strong>tope. It's a pun. I contain multitudes, or at least isotopes.
  </p>
</details>
